RALS - Robot Articulated Links Servo System

RALS is a six DoF manipulator equipped with three-fingered adaptive grasping gripper . It is a 6R manipulator with spherical wrist configuration. The main purpose of this robot is to act as research tool in exploring various aspects of robotic arm. The important features of the robot are:

  1. Compact Design
  2. 6R configuration
  3. Underactuated three-fingered gripper
